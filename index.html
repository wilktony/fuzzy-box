Upcoming Fights

Derek Chisora vs. Otto Wallin

Date: February 8, 2025

Location: Co-op Live, Manchester, England

Details: Veteran heavyweight Derek Chisora faces Sweden's Otto Wallin in a 12-round bout. Chisora, coming off a unanimous decision win over Joe Joyce, aims to continue his momentum against Wallin, who recently secured a first-round KO victory over Onoriode Ehwarieme.

Jack Catterall vs. Arnold Barboza Jr.

Date: February 15, 2025

Location: Manchester, England

Details: In a WBO super lightweight eliminator, Jack Catterall takes on Arnold Barboza Jr. Catterall is coming off a dominant decision victory over Regis Prograis, while Barboza recently defeated Jose Ramirez.

Daniel Dubois vs. Joseph Parker

Date: February 22, 2025

Location: Kingdom Arena, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Details: Daniel Dubois defends his IBF heavyweight title against former WBO champion Joseph Parker. Dubois claimed the title with a knockout victory over Anthony Joshua, while Parker has been on a winning streak, including notable victories over Deontay Wilder and Zhilei Zhang.

Artur Beterbiev vs. Dmitry Bivol

Date: February 22, 2025

Location: Kingdom Arena, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Details: In a highly anticipated rematch, Artur Beterbiev and Dmitry Bivol clash for the undisputed light-heavyweight championship. Beterbiev won their first encounter by majority decision, unifying the WBA, WBC, IBF, and WBO titles.

Shakur Stevenson vs. Floyd Schofield

Date: February 22, 2025

Location: Kingdom Arena, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Details: Shakur Stevenson defends his WBC lightweight title against the undefeated Floyd "Kid Austin" Schofield. Stevenson is known for his exceptional defensive skills, while Schofield is a rising star eager to make his mark.

Gervonta Davis vs. Lamont Roach Jr.

Date: March 1, 2025

Location: Barclays Center, Brooklyn, USA

Details: Gervonta "Tank" Davis defends his WBA lightweight title against Lamont Roach Jr., the reigning WBA super featherweight champion moving up a weight class to challenge Davis.
