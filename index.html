Upcoming Fights

Daniel Dubois vs. Joseph Parker

Date: February 22, 2025

Location: Kingdom Arena, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Details: Daniel Dubois defends his IBF heavyweight title against former WBO champion Joseph Parker. Dubois claimed the title with a knockout victory over Anthony Joshua, while Parker has been on a winning streak, including notable victories over Deontay Wilder and Zhilei Zhang.

Artur Beterbiev vs. Dmitry Bivol

Date: February 22, 2025

Location: Kingdom Arena, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Details: In a highly anticipated rematch, Artur Beterbiev and Dmitry Bivol clash for the undisputed light-heavyweight championship. Beterbiev won their first encounter by majority decision, unifying the WBA, WBC, IBF, and WBO titles.

Shakur Stevenson vs. Floyd Schofield

Date: February 22, 2025

Location: Kingdom Arena, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Details: Shakur Stevenson defends his WBC lightweight title against the undefeated Floyd "Kid Austin" Schofield. Stevenson is known for his exceptional defensive skills, while Schofield is a rising star eager to make his mark.

Carlos Adames vs. Hamzah Sheeraz

Date: February 22, 2025

Location: Kingdom Arena, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Details: WBC middleweight champion Carlos Adames is set to face undefeated British contender Hamzah Sheeraz. Adames aims to defend his title successfully, while Sheeraz looks to make a significant impact on the world stage with this challenging bout.

Vergil Ortiz Jr. vs. Israil Madrimov

Date: February 22, 2025

Location: Kingdom Arena, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Details: In a battle for the WBC interim super welterweight title, hard-hitting Vergil Ortiz Jr. will take on the skilled Uzbekistani boxer Israil Madrimov. Both fighters are known for their aggressive styles, promising an action-packed encounter.

Zhilei Zhang vs. Agit Kabayel

Date: February 22, 2025

Location: Kingdom Arena, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Details: Heavyweight contenders Zhilei Zhang and Agit Kabayel are set to clash for the vacant WBC interim heavyweight title. Zhang, known for his formidable power, faces Kabayel, who brings an undefeated record and tactical prowess to the ring.

Joshua Buatsi vs. Callum Smith

Date: February 22, 2025

Location: Kingdom Arena, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Details: In a compelling matchup for the WBO interim light heavyweight title, British fighters Joshua Buatsi and Callum Smith will face off. Both are eager to secure a title shot against the winner of the Beterbiev-Bivol main event, making this a high-stakes contest.

Gervonta Davis vs. Lamont Roach Jr.

Date: March 1, 2025

Location: Barclays Center, Brooklyn, USA

Details: Gervonta "Tank" Davis defends his WBA lightweight title against Lamont Roach Jr., the reigning WBA super featherweight champion moving up a weight class to challenge Davis.

Callum Walsh vs. Dean Sutherland

Date: March 16, 2025

Location: Hulu Theater at Madison Square Garden, New York, USA

Details: Rising super welterweight star Callum Walsh faces off against Dean Sutherland in a match that promises fireworks.

Skye Nicolson vs. Tiara Brown

Date: March 22, 2025

Location: Sydney, Australia

Details: Skye Nicolson defends her WBC featherweight title against the experienced Tiara Brown in a compelling matchup.

Jesse "Bam" Rodriguez vs. Fernando Martinez

Date: April 12, 2025

Location: AT&T Center, San Antonio, Texas, USA

Details: A unification bout in the super flyweight division as Jesse "Bam" Rodriguez faces Fernando Martinez.

Chris Eubank Jr. vs. Conor Benn

Details: In a bout dubbed "Born Rivals," Chris Eubank Jr. and Conor Benn face off, continuing the legacy of their fathers' storied rivalry.

Canelo Álvarez vs. William Scull
